Our Story

CampusMart was born from a simple observation: students have stuff they don't need, and other students need stuff they don't have. We created a platform to bridge that gap within the trusted community of a faculty.

No complicated sign-ups, no transaction fees, no middlemen. Just students helping students through the power of WhatsApp and campus connections.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CampusMart free to use?

Yes! CampusMart is completely free. There are no listing fees, no transaction fees, and no hidden charges.

How do I contact a seller?

Simply click on any product card to be redirected to the seller's WhatsApp. From there, you can negotiate, ask questions, and arrange a meetup.

How do I list a product?

Go to the Marketplace page and click "Add Your Product". Fill in the details including your WhatsApp number, and your listing will appear immediately.

Is it safe to meet strangers?

We recommend meeting in public places on campus during daylight hours. Bring a friend for larger transactions, and always let someone know where you're going.

Can I delete my listing?

Currently, listings are stored locally. Clear your browser data to remove them, or contact us for assistance.
