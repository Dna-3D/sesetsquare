About Seset Square

Connecting students, one trade at a time

Our Story

Seset Square was born from a simple observation: students have items they no longer need, while other students are actively searching for those same items.

We created a campus-focused marketplace that allows students to buy and sell directly within their faculty using WhatsApp for fast and trusted communication.
